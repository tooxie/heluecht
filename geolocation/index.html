    About Catsei

    Hope you liked it!

    Catsei is just a demo developed by Alvaro Mouriño for the Mozilla Dev Derby.

    It works with geolocation to detect the city you are at a given moment, it then downloads information you may find useful and saves it to read it later, even when lacking an Internet connection.

    Catsei is a full client-side web application, no server required. It's also Free Software so you can download, modify and distribute it's source code.

    Powered by HTML5's Geolocation API, application cache, local storage and dojo.

    Thanks to David Walsh for his great article Creating Mobile Web Applications with dojox.mobile. I have never worked with this technologies before, his post was very useful.
